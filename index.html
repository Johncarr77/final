In 1966 Paul Van Doren & Jimmy Van Doren with Gordon Lee and Serge Delia open a business in Anaheim California.

The first sale would made that morning by twelve customers, eventually leading to the creation of the authentic Vans.

In the early 70's Vans would dominate the skater culture in southern Califorina , by 1976 skaters Stacey Peralta and tony Alva design Vans #95 known as the era.

Vans would release three pairs of new shoes from 1977-1978 with classic trade marks or silhouettes.

These shoes were vans #36 the old skool,vans #98 classic slip on, and style #38 sk8-hi these shoes would be used for different variations later on.

The end of the 1970's would Vans open 70 stores in Califorina, allowing the company to sell nationally and internationally.

By 1984 the company would file for bankruptcy with sells doing good but due to various products resources were low.

The courts approved the file which lead to Paul Van Doren to return as president, with plans on working with cutbacks.

Within three years Vans would pay back all creditors, while getting out of 12 million debt and chapter 11.

In 1991 Vans would offer public stock starting at 14 dollars, this would launch the brand into new business ventures.

From 1995-2001 Vans would sponsor concert events, sporting events and competitions for multiple sports.

The company would celebrate 40 years of success in 2006 with an apparel collection, later on renew Christian Hosoi on releasing limited Hosoi sk8-his

Stories based on iconic action sports legends, artists and musicians would be published as "VANS:OFF THE WALL,STORIES OF SOLE FROM VANS ORIGINALS" in 2009 by Harry N.Abrams.
